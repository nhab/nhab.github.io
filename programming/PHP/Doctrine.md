## What is Doctrine?
Doctrine 2 is an object-relational mapper (ORM) for PHP 5.4
## What are Entities?
Entities are PHP Objects that can be identified by a unique identifier. 

An entity contains <b>persistable</b> properties. <br>
A <b>persistable property</b> is an instance variable of the entity that is __saved into__ and __retrieved from__ the database .

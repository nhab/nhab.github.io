SAP

SAP stands for Systems Applications and Products in Data Processing. SAP by definition is also named of the ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) software as well the name of the company. SAP system consists of a number of fully integrated modules

What is SAP Business Suite?

Is a collection of fully integrated applications such as SAP customer relationship management (CRM), SAP Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP), SAP product lifecycle management (PLM), SAP supplier relationship management (SRM), and SAP supply chain management (SCM) modules.
